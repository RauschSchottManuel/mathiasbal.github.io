Real Friends

Basically, with 'real friends' we mean the people that you interact with on a daily basis in your real life. That means that people who could be living a block away from your place could still be your internet friends if you just hang out online and never see each other in real life.

Real friends are just as old as intelligent life. Generally communication with others has always been important for creatures to survive.

Online Friends

What people usually call internet or online friends are people that you talk to online, usually don't see in real but who you only meet online. It is possible that your friendship can start out as an online one but you can also become friends in real life, for example if one moves closer to the other making it possible to meet each other in person.

You could say internet friends are a occurrence but going by some definitions a pen pal might also just be an internet friend but you write letters to eachother instead of chatting online
